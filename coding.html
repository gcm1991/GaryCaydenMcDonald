Programming Languages

R

Most of my data analysis was done in the R programming language. A typical project involved loading a dataset from a csv or stata file into R, cleaning the data with a variety of tools (dplyr, tidyverse, etc.), visualizing the data (ggplot2), analyzing it through a statistical model (usually linear/logistic regression or ANOVA) and presenting the results in a publication friendly manner.

In addition to my research I taught an undergraduate course (Poli Lab 102) for several semesters. I also served as a teacing aid for Oxford Professor Raymond Duch and was responsible for teaching causal inference in R.
